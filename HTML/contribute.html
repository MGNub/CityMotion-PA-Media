HOW TO CONTRIBUTE

There are several actions any average citizen can take in order to contribute to improving one's city.

  • Vote for politicians/city mayors that support better urban planning.
  • Show up to city council meetings; they are accessible to the public.
  • Form a coalition or alliance group in your neighborhood or community.
  • Promote advocacy for better roads among peers and in your community.
