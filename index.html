Reinventing Cities

Today, the country of America suffers from heavy carcentrism, leading to poor city infrastructure. The cities of America are designed for the car, built around the car, and bulldozed for the car.

There are many issues with American cities, and this website will dive more into the cars, roads, and transportation aspect of urban planning.

The goal of this website is to advocate for, and promote advocacy for better and safer cities in America.
